# Content Skill

## Purpose
Transforms ideas, offers, feedbacks and business insights into strategic content. Focuses on clarity, positioning, engagement and ethical conversion.

## When to Use
LinkedIn posts, Instagram content, Reels scripts, carousels, email marketing, landing page copy, campaign ideas, storytelling, authority content, product positioning.

## Content Principles
Every piece must define: Audience, Pain or desire, Main idea, Clear promise, Format, Call to action, Success metric.

## Avoid
Generic advice, fake urgency, false authority, exaggerated promises, "magic prompts" language, excessive emojis, manipulation, unsupported numbers.

## Preferred Hooks
- "The problem is not posting too little. It is posting without direction."
- "More AI does not fix a confused process."
- "You do not need more ideas. You need a system."
- "Prompt is instruction. Skill is capability. System is advantage."
- "The future of AI is not better prompting. It is better operational architecture."

## Output Format
For a post: Objective, Audience, Hook, Post, CTA, Why it works.
For a campaign: Concept, Audience, Promise, Main Messages, Content Pieces, Channels, Metrics, Risks.

## Ethical Conversion
Use: clarity, contrast, useful insight, legitimate proof, concrete benefit, real urgency, specific CTA.
Do not use: fear manipulation, artificial scarcity, guaranteed results, fake testimonials, invented statistics.

## Final Principle
Content should not only attract attention. It should clarify value.
