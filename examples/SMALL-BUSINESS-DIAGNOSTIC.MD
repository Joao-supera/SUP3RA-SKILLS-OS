# Example — Small Business AI Diagnostic

## Scenario
A small service business receives leads through WhatsApp, Instagram and website forms.
Owner says: "I receive many messages, but I lose track of clients and forget to follow up."

## Diagnosis Summary
The business has a lead management and follow-up problem, not only a messaging problem.
Leads arrive through multiple channels without consistent process to classify, track, prioritize and follow up.

## Main Bottleneck
Absence of structured lead pipeline. Messages arrive but are not converted into lead records, status, priority, next action, follow-up reminder, or sales opportunity.

## Root Cause Hypothesis
Operational fragmentation: too many channels, no central lead list, no standard response, no follow-up routine, no visibility of pending opportunities.

## AI Opportunity
First opportunity is a Lead Management Agent that can:
- summarize each lead;
- classify intent;
- detect urgency;
- suggest a response;
- recommend next action;
- create follow-up reminders;
- flag cases for human review.

## Recommended First Agent
Agent Name: Lead Management Agent
Purpose: Organize incoming leads and reduce forgotten opportunities.
Main Tasks: classify lead intent, summarize conversation, suggest reply, identify urgency, recommend follow-up, flag sensitive cases.
Human Review Required: Yes, before sending commercial promises, discounts or sensitive responses.

## Risks
Wrong lead classification, unsupported promises, exposing personal data, automating responses without review, sending generic replies that damage trust.

## Governance Notes
AI should not: approve discounts alone, make legal/financial promises, store unnecessary sensitive data, send final replies in high-risk cases, replace sales team.
AI should: suggest, summarize, classify, organize, escalate, log.

## Suggested 30-Day MVP
Week 1: Map 20 most common customer questions and define lead stages.
Week 2: Create basic knowledge base and response templates.
Week 3: Build simple Lead Management Agent for classification and reply suggestions.
Week 4: Run controlled pilot and measure response time, handoff rate, forgotten leads.

## Success Metrics
Track: first response time, number of forgotten leads, follow-up completion rate, handoff rate to humans, classification accuracy, sales team satisfaction.

## Final Recommendation
Start with simple lead diagnosis and one agent. Do not build complete SaaS before validating workflow.
The first goal is not full automation. The first goal is operational clarity.
